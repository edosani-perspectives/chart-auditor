Prevention Impact Analysis

Key Finding: Implementing a comprehensive chart audit system can prevent 3,111 denials (31.6%) by catching documentation and compliance issues before claims are submitted.

This analysis maps each denial category to specific chart audit criteria that would prevent those denials. By systematically auditing charts against these criteria, clinics can catch errors before they result in claim denials or payment delays.

Prevention Impact by Audit Area

The following chart shows how many denials each audit area can prevent. Areas are ranked by total prevention impact:

Audit Area Details

Timeliness Documentation Requirements

Prevents: 1,488 denials

Categories Impacted: Timeliness (1,322 denials), Information (166 denials)

Key Audit Criteria (9 checks):

  • Was BPS completed within 24 hours of admission?
  • Was initial treatment plan started within 24 hours?
  • Was Case Management Needs Assessment completed within 72 hours?
  • Initial Clinical Note within 7 days of admission?
  • Are there Clinical Notes every 7 days?
  • Were those notes completed the same day of service?
  • Was family contacted within 72 hours of admission?
  • Initial Peer Progress Note completed within 72 hours?
  • Are Peer Progress Notes completed every 7 to 14 days?

Authorization & Pre-certification Tracking

Prevents: 761 denials

Categories Impacted: Referral/Pre-authorization (761 denials)

Key Audit Criteria (4 checks):

  • Is the VOB attached? (shows auth requirements)
  • Track utilization review dates and authorization expiration
  • Alert when authorization is approaching expiration
  • Ensure services provided match authorized level of care

Medical Necessity Documentation

Prevents: 410 denials

Categories Impacted: Medical Necessity (410 denials)

Key Audit Criteria (8 checks):

  • Does the Clinical Note provide medical necessity?
  • Are the diagnoses explained by the Psych Eval?
  • Does the treatment plan in the evaluation reflect the needs of the member?
  • Does the Clinical Note clearly reflect treatment goals?
  • Do Treatment Plan problems reflect problems identified in the BPS?
  • Is each goal written as a SMART goal?
  • Are outcome measures updated to reflect progress?
  • Are there specific barriers listed for step-down to lower level of care?

Required Documentation Attachments

Prevents: 335 denials

Categories Impacted: Doc./Info requests (170 denials), Information (165 denials)

Key Audit Criteria (7 checks):

  • Is the Pre-Admission Assessment attached?
  • Is the VOB attached?
  • If applicable, are medical records attached?
  • Are all consents completed and signed?
  • Are all ROIs completed and signed?
  • Is the member's insurance card uploaded to chart?
  • Is the member's photo ID uploaded to the chart?

Level of Care Documentation

Prevents: 98 denials

Categories Impacted: Place of Service (98 denials)

Key Audit Criteria (3 checks):

  • Is each section of ASAM completed appropriately?
  • Does MSE reflect information provided in ASAM?
  • Are there specific barriers listed for step-down to lower level of care?

Coding Support Documentation

Prevents: 19 denials

Categories Impacted: Coding (19 denials)

Key Audit Criteria (3 checks):

  • Are the diagnoses explained and supported by documentation?
  • Does documentation support the procedure codes billed?
  • Is the level of care clearly documented?
