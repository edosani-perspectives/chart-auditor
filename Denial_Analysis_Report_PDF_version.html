Overall Prevention Coverage

Summary: The chart audit system can prevent 3,111 denials (31.6%) through documentation completeness checks. The largest impact comes from timeliness requirements (1,488 denials prevented) and authorization tracking (761 denials prevented).

Note on Credentialing/Enrollment (6,736 denials): This category is marked as "Unclear" in prevention analysis. While chart audits may catch some credentialing issues indirectly (e.g., verifying provider information), these denials are primarily administrative and may require separate credentialing verification processes beyond clinical chart audits.
